Data Dictionary / Glossary

PE (Program Element)
Unique identifier for each RDT&E program in the DoD budget. Format: 7 digits + 1-2 letter suffix (e.g., 0603285E).
Fiscal Year (FY)
DoD fiscal year runs October 1 through September 30. FY2026 = Oct 2025 - Sep 2026.
Actual
Amounts actually obligated by the end of the fiscal year. Reflects real spending.
Enacted
Amounts signed into law by Congress for a given fiscal year.
Request
The President's budget request submitted to Congress. May differ from enacted amounts.
Appropriation
The "color of money" â€” the legal authority to spend. Categories include RDT&E, Procurement, O&M, MILPERS, and MILCON.
RDT&E
Research, Development, Test & Evaluation. Covers basic research through operational testing.
Exhibit Type
Budget document format. R-2: RDT&E program details. P-5: procurement line items. O-1: operations & maintenance. P-1/R-1: summary tables.
Amount Unit ($K)
All monetary values are in thousands of dollars unless otherwise noted.
Budget Activity
Broad category within an appropriation (e.g., "Basic Research" under RDT&E).
Organization / Service
The military service or defense agency submitting the budget: Army, Navy, Air Force, Space Force, Marine Corps, or Defense-Wide.
Supplemental
Additional funding enacted outside the normal budget cycle, typically for urgent or emergency needs.
Reconciliation
Adjustments made to align budget figures across different documents and time periods.
